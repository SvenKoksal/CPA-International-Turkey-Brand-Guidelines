The Ecosystem
01

Two brands, one ecosystem

CPA International Group operates two complementary advisory brands; CPA International Turkey and DETR Consulting are sister brands in a single cross-border advisory ecosystem. They are not interchangeable, and they are not a single master brand — each is its own firm, with its own domain, audience and role.

This is not an endorsed-house with a parent logo. There is no group wordmark, no “Part of…” endorsement mark. The brands are connected by a defined role split, a shared design language, an intentional cross-referencing model, and their common membership in the Delphi Alliance network.

CPA International Turkey

The primary authority. The established practice on the ground in Istanbul and the ecosystem’s operating engine — the broad, deep source of technical and regulatory expertise across Turkish tax, accounting, payroll, audit, transfer pricing and CFO work. Predominantly Turkey-centred, serving local and international clients.

Operating authorityDepth & breadth
DETR Consulting

The cross-border front-end. The Berlin-based, Germany-facing gateway — a focused, high-intent brand for foreign investors evaluating and structuring their entry into the Turkish market. Narrower by design. Not a second, lighter version of CPA International Turkey.

Hierarchy
02

Roles & boundaries

The two brands are not equal twins. CPA International Turkey is the primary authority domain that carries the ecosystem’s depth; DETR is the focused conversion layer for one specific journey — Germany into Türkiye.

Primary · Authority & Operating Domain

Broad operational + technical depth · the content & expertise engine

Focused · Cross-Border Conversion Front-End

Germany→Türkiye market entry · feeds operational work to CPA International Turkey

Don’t mirror

DETR must never reproduce CPA International Turkey’s full service catalogue. Where depth is needed, it refers to CPA International Turkey.

Don’t compete

The brands must not chase the same keywords or run parallel versions of the same message — that risks cannibalisation.

Do complement

CPA International Turkey wins topical breadth and authority; DETR wins a narrow, high-value cross-border journey.

Separation
03

Topic ownership

To prevent overlap, every theme has a single clear owner. This is the most important boundary in the ecosystem.

CPA International Turkey owns — operational & technical depth
  • — Turkish tax advisory & compliance
  • — Accounting & bookkeeping
  • — Payroll & social-security (SSI)
  • — Audit & assurance
  • — Transfer pricing
  • — Outsourced CFO & financial reporting / IFRS
  • — Regulatory & tax updates (the content engine)
DETR owns — cross-border market entry
  • — Market entry into Türkiye
  • — Company formation for foreign / German investors
  • — Germany–Türkiye business structuring
  • — Setup for foreign-owned Turkish companies
  • — Cross-border advisory scenarios
  • — Investor-facing, conversion-oriented pages

Shared themes — company formation, financial consulting, foreign-investor support — are the danger zone. When both could plausibly cover a topic, DETR frames the cross-border decision; CPA International Turkey executes the operational detail.

The Shared DNA
04

Shared design DNA

With no parent logo, the kinship is carried entirely by a shared design language. Three systems are common to both brands — the quiet thread that makes them feel related.

A shared neutral spine

Used identically by both brands

Both firms set type and lay out documents on the same warm-paper neutrals — same ink, same paper, same greys.

One type pairing, two emphases

Spectral + Archivo

Both draw from the same two typefaces. CPA International Turkey leads with the structured sans (Archivo); DETR leads with the classical serif (Spectral) — echoing each logo.

A common layout rhythm

Grid, rules & numbering

Numbered chapters, hairline rules, tracked uppercase kickers, generous white space on warm paper. Each brand keeps its own colour; the rhythm stays constant.

Note: the two brands’ colours stay fully distinct — CPA International Turkey navy, DETR’s German tricolor. Colour is never shared; structure always is.

Connection
05

Cross-referencing

Instead of a shared endorsement mark, the brands connect by pointing to each other at the right moment in a client’s journey — an intentional cross-referencing model, not co-branding.

On DETR materials → refer to CPA International Turkey
Once on the ground in Türkiye
Operational tax, audit & accounting by CPA International Turkey

When the journey moves from decision to execution, DETR hands off to CPA International Turkey.

On CPA International Turkey materials → refer to DETR
Entering from Germany?
Cross-border market entry via DETR Consulting, Berlin

CPA International Turkey references DETR only for the clearly Germany-to-Türkiye entry journey.

Stewardship
07

Governance

What’s shared
Neutral palette, type pairing (Spectral + Archivo) and layout rhythm only. Nothing else.
What’s never shared
Colour, logo, a “group” mark, or a combined lockup. There is no master brand.
Boundary rule
Before publishing, ask: does this topic belong to CPA International Turkey or DETR? One owner only — see §03.
Cross-links
Reference the sister brand only at the natural hand-off point in the client journey.
