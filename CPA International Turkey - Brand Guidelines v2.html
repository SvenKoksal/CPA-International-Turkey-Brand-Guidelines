Brand at a Glance
01

Who we are

CPA International Turkey is the primary authority of the cross-border ecosystem — the established Istanbul practice and operating engine for tax, audit, accounting, payroll and finance in Türkiye, serving local and international companies with depth, rigour and breadth.

Role in the ecosystem

Operating authority & content engine. CPA International Turkey carries the technical and regulatory depth — the broad service catalogue and the expertise the whole ecosystem relies on. It is primarily Turkey-centred; where a client’s journey clearly begins in Germany, CPA International Turkey refers them to its sister brand, DETR Consulting.

Tagline

Think differently, together — shaping a successful future.

Primary line in English. Turkish and German equivalents are used on the respective language sites.

Audience & language

CPA International Turkey’s audience is predominantly Turkish-speaking and Turkey-centred (Istanbul, Ankara, Izmir), with a growing international layer. Lead with Turkish and English; German is one supported language of three, not the default voice.

Identity
02

The logo

The logo pairs a circular badge — a stepped monument rising under stars over a horizon — with a clean, capitalised sans-serif wordmark. The badge stands for solidity; the stars for aspiration; the circle for a complete, governed practice.

Primary lockup

Horizontal lockup — the default. Use wherever space allows.

The badge

For avatars, favicons, app icons and stamps where the wordmark won’t be legible.

Reversed

On ink or navy, the wordmark knocks out to white; the badge keeps its colour.

Background discipline

White and warm paper are preferred. On dark or navy, always use the reversed lockup.

Graphic Language
07

Graphic language

Two motifs extend the logo into layouts: the four-point star and the horizon line. Used sparingly, they make any CPA International Turkey artefact recognisable without the full logo.

The star

A four-point star marks excellence, headings and section breaks — on navy, in white or silver.

The horizon

A single horizon line splits navy from deep indigo — a grounding device for covers and dividers.

Verbal
09

Voice & tone

We sound like a senior advisor: precise, plain-spoken and reassuring. We explain complexity without inflating it — and we never blur into DETR’s cross-border pitch.

Clear

Short sentences. Plain terms before jargon. Define the technical word once, then use it.

Measured

Confident, never boastful. We state what we do and stand behind it.

Useful

Every paragraph helps the reader act. We lead with the decision, not the disclaimer.

We write
We avoid
  • “We prepare your monthly accounts so you always know where you stand.”
  • “Here’s what the new circular changes — and what you need to do.”
  • “Filed on time, every time.”
  • “We leverage synergistic best-in-class solutions.”
  • Positioning CPA International Turkey as a Germany-entry specialist — that is DETR’s role.
  • Walls of regulation with no takeaway.
